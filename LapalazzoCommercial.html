Designed for large scale corporate offices, La Palazzo Commercial is built as a Grade A, LEED certified (under execution) office space. With large 30,000 sq ft floor plates and a 11m span between columns, it allows for high efficiency occupation. Located on the Sarjapur Road - Outer Ring Road Junction, La Palazzo Commercial is located in the new IT corridor, and easily accessible to residential enclaves in the area.
